Welcome! My name is Jiashuo Jiang and I am an assistant professor of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Prior to joining HKUST, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics (2017) from Peking University and my PhD degree (2022) in Operations from NYU Stern School of Business, advised by Prof. Jiawei Zhang and Prof. Will Ma. During my PhD, I have also done an internship at Amazon and been a visiting graduate student at the Simons Institute.

I am broadly interested in online algorithms, online learning, stochastic optimization and stochastic control problems, arising from the application areas such as supply chain management, inventory management, revenue management and mechanism design.
